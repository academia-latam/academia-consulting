# AcademIA — Product / Brand brief (impeccable context)

**Register:** brand / marketing site (design IS the product).
**What it is:** the public website of AcademIA, an AI-in-education consultancy serving universities, schools, and faculty in Mexico. Founder: Hesus Garcia Cobos.
**Audience:** academic leaders (rectors, deans, academic directors), faculty, and institutions evaluating responsible AI adoption.
**Goal:** look credible and modern enough to (a) win real institutional clients and (b) corroborate the founder's profile for a Hubert H. Humphrey Fellowship application.
**Languages:** English + Spanish (Spanish is the primary market; English supports the fellowship/international audience).
**Services:** (1) Faculty AI training, (2) Curriculum design, (3) AI-literacy courses, (4) AI policy / governance advisory.
**Proof points:** DeepLearning.AI / Coursera curriculum work; the open, UNESCO-aligned *ai-for-teachers* course; IIEP-UNESCO policy training; comparative AI-in-education policy research.
**Contact:** info@academiaconsulting.com
**Tech:** dependency-free static HTML/CSS/JS (no build step) → deploys to Vercel; custom domain (likely academiaconsulting.com).

## Brand direction
- **Mood:** "morning light in a university hall — clear, blue, exact." Trustworthy, intelligent, human; not corporate-cold, not edu-cute.
- **Color strategy:** Committed — a confident azure carries identity; pure-white surface; sparing warm amber accent.
- **Palette (OKLCH):** primary azure `oklch(0.50 0.15 252)`, accent amber `oklch(0.76 0.15 78)`, bg pure white `oklch(1 0 0)`, ink `oklch(0.22 0.02 252)`.
- **Type:** Fraunces (serif display) + Inter (sans body) — paired on the serif/sans contrast axis.
- **Avoid:** gradient text, glassmorphism, identical icon-card grids, uppercase tracked eyebrows on every section, AI-navy-cream-orange cliche.
