FIG. 06 — WHAT WE BELIEVE

We don't ban
the calculator.
We teach the math.

AI in education isn't a productivity hack or a moral panic. It's a literacy. We treat it like one: taught, assessed, governed, and grounded in what actually happens in a classroom. AI can write the draft; it can't see a student and know something is off. We protect that part — we don't automate it.
